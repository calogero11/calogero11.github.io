HOW IT ALL STARTED...

Imagine...Imagine this. Dave is a incredibly motivated student and he wants to achieve top results and so do his parents but, he's at a little disadvantage. He has no technology and his parents wants to keep everything old school nevertheless the more he put effort in, the more he spent time revising, the more inefficient he was compared to his friends where they could find information rapidly instead Dave was at home wasting time through book, trying his best to find the precised information but still incapable to keep up. Do you really want to be a Dave?

When I was a child I didn't understand the power of technology until I gave my first instruction to a machine; print('Hello world'). Since then my passion to programming has been crescendo and iv'e been acknowledging the technology around me programmed to help us, to make our everyday tasks easier and more efficient. In addition I knew if I wanted to be a developer and achieve my dreams I had to be ahead of my game as a developer's life is tremendously competitive...
